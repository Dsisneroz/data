Gapminder Data

Description:

     Health and income outcomes for 184 countries from 1960 to 2016.
     Also includes two character vectors, ‘oecd’ and ‘opec’, with the
     names of OECD and OPEC countries from 2016.

Usage:

    read.csv("")

Format:

     An object of class ‘"data.frame"’.

Details:

        • country.

        • year.

        • infant_mortality. Infant deaths per 1000.

        • life_expectancy. Life expectancy in years.

        • fertility. Average number of children per woman.

        • population. Country population.

        • gpd. GDP according to World Bankdev.

        • continent.

        • region. Geographical region.

Examples:

    read.csv("")
