MLB                 package:openintro                  R Documentation

Salary data for Major League Baseball (2010)

Description:

     Salary data for Major League Baseball players in the year 2010.

Usage:

     data(MLB)
     
Format:

     A data frame with 828 observations on the following 4 variables.

     ‘player’ Player name

     ‘team’ Team

     ‘position’ Field position

     ‘salary’ Salary (in $1000s)

Source:

     Collected from the following page (and its linked pages) on
     February 23rd, 2011:

     <
     http://content.usatoday.com/sportsdata/baseball/mlb/salaries/team>
